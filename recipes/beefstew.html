Beefstew, the Flemish way



Story

Beefstew might be the pinnacle of Flemish cuisine.
Historically seen as a way to make mediocre meat delicious. However, today it is seen as a luxury dish.
This is most likely due to the time it takes to prepare having become a more valuable comodity.

If you follow this recipe you will end up with an amazing and tasty take on stew.
Although one should consider this to take around two to three hours.


Ingredients for 4 persons

  • Beef: Preferably chuck roast, cut in cubes
  • Beer: Two bottles of dark belgian beer to your liking
  • Bouquet Garni: Bind together the following:
    • 3 sticks of Thyme
    • 2 Bay leaves
    • 3 sticks of Parsley
